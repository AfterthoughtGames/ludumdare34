Cornflower Corban

Our team decided to go with growing for this Ludum Dare. The player, a shark named Cornflower Corban grows the more he shoots and then shrinks when you stop shooting. We ended up settling on making the game a side scroller, a genre that each of us have played over the years. Thank you for taking a look at our game, we hope you enjoy playing!

Story

One day Cornflower Corban was having a relaxing swim through the ocean when he got word that there would be an attack from the neighboring village. Corban decided to prepare for the purple brigade so he could defend all he loved. He grabbed the biggest laser turret he could find and strapped it to his back so he could have a fighting chance. Corban needs your help to fend off the enemy attacks! Be careful though because with each shot Corban gets bigger and its harder for him to move, so use your shots wisely!

Gameplay

Cornflower Corban is a side scrolling bullet hell. The further right you move the faster the enemies spawn. The more you shoot the bigger corban gets, and the bigger he gets the slower he moves. If you stop shooting corban gets smaller.

Keyboard:

  • W: Move up
  • A: Move Left
  • S: Move Down
  • D: Move Right
  • Space: Fire
  • F1: Debug mode
  • Esc: Exit

Xbox Controler:

  • Left stick: Move
  • A: Fire
  • Right stick press: Debug mode
  • Back: Exit
